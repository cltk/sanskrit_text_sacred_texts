kathaṃ nu saraṇe 'śaktān patane ca mamātmajān

jaritāriḥ kathaṃ putraḥ sārisṛkvaḥ kathaṃ ca me

lālapyamānaṃ tam ṛṣiṃ mandapālaṃ tathā vane
